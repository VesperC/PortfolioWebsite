First Emperor's Mausoleum

2022 - 2023 / Dawning Game Studio / Unity URP

This is a puzzle VR tomb adventure game. The protagonist is an archaeology student who enters Qin Shi Huang (the first emperor of Qin dynasty)'s tomb by mistake and encounters many dangerous traps. He eventually escapes with the help of  of a pottery figurine possessed by the spirit of an ancient girl. The game is still in development.

Personal Contribution: A wide range of art-related content, includes 2D art, 3D models, animations, and shaders.

The game has five levels: gold, wood, water, fire, and earth, which are the basic five elements in Chinese philosophy. Depending on the elemental theme, each level has different puzzles to solve.

The game is still in development.
